EXAMINATIONS
------------
A minimum of 75% attendance is required to be eligible to sit for the
end-semester examinations. Students below 75% may apply for condonation
with a valid medical certificate. To apply for re-evaluation of an
answer sheet, submit the re-evaluation form within 10 days of the result
declaration, along with a fee of Rs. 300 per subject.

SCHOLARSHIPS
------------

the previous semester and have no pending disciplinary action. The
application window opens in the first week of each semester.
